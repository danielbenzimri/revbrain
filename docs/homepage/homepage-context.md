# RevBrain Homepage Context

> Marketing positioning and target audience documentation for the RevBrain platform.

## Platform Overview

**RevBrain** is a construction project management and quantity surveying platform designed specifically for the Israeli infrastructure and construction market. The platform digitizes the traditionally manual, error-prone process of quantity calculations, bill verification, and project execution tracking.

### Core Value Proposition

> "Transform weeks of manual calculation work into hours of verified, traceable results."

---

## Target Audiences

### 1. Quantity Surveyors & Contractors (חשב כמויות / קבלן / משרדי תכנון)

**Who they are:**

- Professional quantity surveyors (חשבי כמויות)
- Construction contractors preparing execution bills
- Engineering and planning offices
- Infrastructure companies (roads, water, drainage)

**Their pain points:**

- Manual calculations from CAD/DXF files take days or weeks
- Excel spreadsheets are error-prone and hard to audit
- No traceability between drawings and final quantities
- Version control nightmares when plans change
- Difficult to defend calculations during disputes

**What they need from RevBrain:**

- **20 Calculation Modules** covering all infrastructure disciplines:
  - Earthworks (עבודות עפר) - cut/fill calculations
  - Paving (ריצוף וסלילה) - areas, thicknesses, structures
  - Retaining Walls (קירות תמך) - gravity, reinforced, MSE, cladding
  - Drainage & Pipes (צנרת וניקוז) - lengths, diameters, manholes
  - Landscaping (פיתוח סביבתי) - green areas, irrigation
  - Street Lighting (תאורת רחוב) - poles, cables, panels
  - And 14 more specialized modules...

- **CAD Integration** - Import DXF files, extract quantities directly from drawings
- **Automatic BoQ Generation** - Bill of Quantities with full traceability
- **Version Tracking** - Know exactly what changed between revisions
- **Export to Excel** - Formatted reports ready for submission

**Key message:**

> "מחשבים כמויות? עברו מאקסל ל-RevBrain. חישובים מדויקים, מעקב שינויים, ודוחות מוכנים להגשה."

---

### 2. Inspectors & Controllers (מפקח / בקר)

**Who they are:**

- Site inspectors (מפקחי עבודה)
- Project controllers on behalf of clients/municipalities
- Government supervisors (משרד השיכון, רמ"י, עיריות)
- Third-party verification consultants

**Their pain points:**

- Receiving thick folders of calculations with no way to verify
- Can't trace how final numbers were derived
- Manual comparison between submitted bills and approved plans
- No visibility into calculation methodology
- Disputes take months to resolve

**What they need from RevBrain:**

- **Read-only access** to contractor calculations
- **Visual verification** - see calculations overlaid on drawings
- **Audit trail** - full history of changes and who made them
- **Notes & Comments** - request clarifications directly in the system
- **Approval workflow** - approve/reject line items with documentation
- **Photo evidence** - attach site photos to verify quantities
- **Comparison tools** - compare submitted vs. approved quantities

**Key message:**

> "קיבלתם חשבון ביצוע? בדקו אותו ב-RevBrain. כל הנתונים, התמונות והחישובים - במקום אחד."

---

### 3. Tender Estimates (אומדנים למכרזים)

**Who they are:**

- Contractors bidding on public tenders
- Estimation departments at construction companies
- Consultants preparing preliminary budgets
- Project managers assessing feasibility

**Their pain points:**

- Tight deadlines for tender submissions (2-4 weeks typical)
- Need rough quantities fast, without full detailed design
- Historical data scattered across old projects
- No benchmarks for unit costs
- Risk of significant under/over estimation

**What they need from RevBrain:**

- **Quick estimation mode** - rough quantities from preliminary drawings
- **Historical comparisons** - similar past projects as benchmarks
- **Risk buffers** - automatic contingency calculations
- **Unit cost database** - market rates for common items
- **Fast turnaround** - days instead of weeks

**Use case:**

1. Receive tender documents
2. Upload preliminary drawings to RevBrain
3. Get rough quantities within 1-2 days
4. Apply unit costs and margins
5. Submit competitive bid with confidence

**Key message:**

> "מכרז בעוד שבועיים? קבלו אומדן כמויות מהיר ומבוסס על נתוני עבר - בימים ולא בשבועות."

---

### 4. Managed Service (שירות מלא)

**Who they are:**

- Small contractors without in-house quantity surveyors
- Companies with overflow work during peak seasons
- Organizations wanting to outsource non-core activities
- Projects requiring certified/independent calculations

**Their pain points:**

- Can't afford full-time quantity surveyor
- Quality varies with freelancers
- No standardization across projects
- Communication gaps with external consultants
- No visibility into work progress

**What RevBrain offers:**

- **Full outsourced service** - we do the calculations for you
- **Platform transparency** - see progress in real-time on the system
- **Certified results** - professional review and sign-off
- **Fixed pricing** - predictable costs per project/module
- **Fast turnaround** - dedicated team with platform expertise

**Service tiers:**
| Tier | Description | Turnaround |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Express | Single module, priority handling | 2-3 days |
| Standard | Full project calculations | 1-2 weeks |
| Enterprise | Ongoing relationship, dedicated team | As needed |

**Key message:**

> "אין לכם חשב כמויות? אנחנו נעשה את העבודה, ואתם תראו הכל במערכת - בשקיפות מלאה."
